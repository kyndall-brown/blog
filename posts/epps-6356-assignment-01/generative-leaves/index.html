{axiom=“X”} rules=list(“X”=“F-[[X]+X]+F[+FX]-X”, “F”=“FF+1”) angle=23 depth=7

I changed the angle to 23, but also the depth to 7 and added +1 to “F” in the axiom variable. The result was shocking:
